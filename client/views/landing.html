What is Helper?

Helper is a different type of social network: one built around you helping others. Be it your friends, family, church, or neighborhood, there are people in your communities that need you and your skills today and Helper wants to help you ensure that you are able to help them when and where you can.

Built around the concept that we are better together, Helper is designed to encourage action over "thoughts and prayers", to build up others instead of ourselves, to use our technologies and freedoms to leave our world better than we came into it. We are building Helper because we believe that our Groups are more important than us as individuals and want to help others find out how they can be helpful.

Whether it's knowing that your neighbor needs their lawn mowed next week or a fundraiser for a recent widow, all over our communities there are people in great need that we believe is up to us to fulfill. Helper ensures that no one falls through your support network's cracks, that every need is fulfilled and everyone feels a common sense of community.

We believe that through God we can find purpose and through helping we can find God. Request access for your community today and see how Helper can help you help others.

Support

Helper ensures that your group is getting the support it needs by bringing to light the people and needs that are already in your communities while giving each member the ability and autonomy to help however and whenever they can.

Do the people that know how to woodwork know that Sally needs a new deck? Do the prayer leaders know that Joe is in need of supernatural healing? Helper ensures that all of the needs of your groups are raised and assigned to a Helper, ensuring that not only is each of us helping but that each of us is getting the help we need.
